Astera Labs IPO Pricing

March 19, 2024 - Astera Labs announced today that their initial public offering (IPO) will be priced at $36 per share.

The company aims to raise around $712.8 million through this offering of 19.8 million shares.

The gross proceeds are expected to be approximately $712,800,000 before underwriting fees.

Net proceeds after deducting underwriter discounts will be around $667,300,000.

The underwriter discount totals approximately $45,500,000.
